About Blendo

We started Blendo because we thought startups deserved better marketing.

Not more marketing. Better marketing. The kind that actually shows up in your revenue — not just your dashboard.

Built for startups that move fast.

Most agencies are built for big brands with big budgets and long timelines. That's fine for them — but it doesn't work for startups. You need speed, transparency, and results you can point to when your investors ask what's working.

Blendo was built to fill that gap. We're a performance marketing agency that works at startup speed — fast iterations, honest reporting, and a relentless focus on the metrics that actually matter: revenue, ROAS, and cost per acquisition.

We don't do vanity metrics. We don't do 6-month "strategy phases." We do results.

What we believe
Six principles that guide everything we do.

These aren't things we put on a wall. They're how we actually work — every day, with every client.

01

Transparency over everything

You'll always know where your money goes and what it's doing. No hidden fees, no confusing reports, no surprises.

02

Speed is a feature

Startups can't wait three weeks for a campaign to launch. We move fast because your growth depends on it.

03

Data decides, not egos

We test everything and let the numbers tell us what works. Opinions are great — but data is better.

04

Partners, not vendors

We embed in your team. Your Slack, your calls, your goals. When you win, we win.

05

Honest when it hurts

If something's not working, we'll tell you — and fix it. We'd rather lose a client than waste their money.

06

Keep it simple

No jargon, no unnecessary complexity. We explain things clearly because you have better things to do than decode marketing speak.
