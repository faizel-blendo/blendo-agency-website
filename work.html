The challenge

[Client Name] had been running Google Ads in-house for over a year. They were spending €14K per month but couldn't tell which campaigns were driving real revenue. Their tracking was broken, their account structure was messy, and they suspected a large chunk of their budget was being wasted — but didn't know where.

What we did

We started with a full account audit and tracking overhaul. We rebuilt the campaign structure around intent tiers, fixed conversion attribution, and cut all underperforming keywords. Then we launched a testing framework — new ad copy, landing page variants, and bid strategies — optimising weekly based on real revenue data, not just clicks.
