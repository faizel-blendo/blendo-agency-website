## Values
**Label:** What we believe
**Headline:** Six principles that guide everything we do.
**Intro:** These aren't things we put on a wall. They're how we actually work — every day, with every client.

1. **Transparency over everything** — You'll always know where your money goes and what it's doing. No hidden fees, no confusing reports, no surprises.
2. **Speed is a feature** — Startups can't wait three weeks for a campaign to launch. We move fast because your growth depends on it.
3. **Data decides, not egos** — We test everything and let the numbers tell us what works. Opinions are great — but data is better.
4. **Partners, not vendors** — We embed in your team. Your Slack, your calls, your goals. When you win, we win.
5. **Honest when it hurts** — If something's not working, we'll tell you — and fix it. We'd rather lose a client than waste their money.
6. **Keep it simple** — No jargon, no unnecessary complexity. We explain things clearly because you have better things to do than decode marketing speak.
